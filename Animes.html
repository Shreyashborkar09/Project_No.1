Animes



Attack on Titan

Genre: Mystrey, Action, Thriller.

Storyline: After his hometown is destroyed and his mother is killed, young
Eren Jaeger vows to cleanse the earth of the giant humanoid Titans that
have brought humanity to the brink of extinction.

Starring: Yuki Kaji, Marina Inoue, Josh Grelle.

Code Geass

Genre: Supernatural, Thriller

Storyline: After being given a mysterious power to control others, an
outcast prince becomes the masked leader of the rebellion against
an all powerful empire

Starring: Jun Fukuyama, johnny Yong Bosch, Yuri Lowenthal.

Death Note

Genre: Supernatural, Thriller

Storyline: An intelligent high school student goes on a secret crusade to
eliminate criminals from the world after discovering a notebook capable
of killing anyone whose name is written into it.

Starring: Mamoru Miyano, Brad Swaile, Vincent Tong.



Demon Slayer

Genre: Action, Supernatural

Storyline: A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive -
Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro
sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his
sister.

Starring: Natsuki Hanae, Zach Aguilar, Abby Trott

One Punch Man

Genre: Comedy, Action

Storyline: The story of Saitama, a hero that does it just for fun & can
defeat his enemies with a single punch.

Starring: Makoto Furukawa, Kaito Ishikawa, Max Mittelman.

Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Genre: Action, Thriller

Storyline: Two brothers search for a Philosopher's Stone after an attempt
to revive their deceased mother goes away and leaves them in damaged
physical forms.

Starring: Kent Williams, Lemasa Kayumi, Vic Mignogna



Jujutsu Kaisen

Genre: Supernatural, Action

Storyline: A boy swallows a cursed talisman - the finger of a demon - and
becomes cursed himself. He enters a shaman's school to be able to locate
the demon's other body parts and thus exorcise himself.

Starring: Junya Enoki, Yuichi Nakamura, Yuma Uchida.

Mob Psycho 100

Genre: Supernatural, Comedy, Action

Storyline: A psychic middle school boy tries to live a normal life and keep
his growing powers under control, even though he constantly gets into
trouble.

Starring: Stsuo Iti, Takahiro Sakurai, Akio Otsuka.

One Piece

Genre: Adventure, Action, Comedy

Storyline: Follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew
in order to find the greatest treasure ever left by the legendary Pirate, Gold
Roger. The famous mystery treasure named "One Piece".

Starring: Mayumi Tanaka, Tony Beck, Laurent Vernin.



Parasyte-the maxim

Genre: Action, Thriller

Storyline: 17-year-old Shinichi Izumi is partially infected by a Parasyte;
monsters that butcher and consume humans. He must learn to co-exist
with the creature if he is to survive both the life of a Parasyte and human,
as part monster, part person.

Starring: Adams Gibbs, Aya Hirano, Brittney Karbowski.

Your Name

Genre: Romance, Drama

Storyline: Two strangers find themselves linked in a bizarre way. When a
connection forms, will distance be the only thing to keep them apart?

Starring: Ryunosuke Kamiki, Mone Kamishiraishi, Ryo Narita.

Steins Gate

Genre: Sci-fi, Thriller, Mystery

Storyline: After discovering time travel, a university student and his
colleagues must use their knowledge of it to stop an evil organization and
their diabolical plans.

Starring: Mamoru Miyano, Asami Imai, Kana Hanazawa.
